## Assembly Instructions
- Use PLA filament with any layer height
- The top and bottom parts fit together without glue
- For improved stability, add a 100 g lead weight inside the enclosure
- It is recommended to attach silicone feet to the bottom
- The opening in `bottom.stl` is designed for a Molex power connector
